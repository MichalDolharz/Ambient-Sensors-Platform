Zone constructor. Creates desired zone at desired placed and adds it to the scene.

Parameters
[out]sceneScene handle. Created zone is added to this scene.
[in]xx coordinate, at which the zone will be created.
[in]yy coordinate, at which the zone will be created.
[in]zoneNumberNumber of zone, where 0 is the red one and 3 is the green one. Data correctness is not being checked. Amount of blocks and their color is based on this parameter.
[in]zonePositionNumber of position of zone to be created, where 0 is the farthest left one and <4> is the farthest right one. Rotation and position of a zone is based on this parameter.
[in]viewModeView, which zone being created will be part of.
